About

Daniel Durgin

I'm the owner and fabricator behind Q Fab Works, based in Myrtle Beach, South Carolina. I'm a retired U.S. Army Staff Sergeant with 18 years of service, and I hold Advanced Welding Technology certifications from Horry-Georgetown Technical College alongside a background in digital arts and graphic design.

That combination is the shop: welding and metal fabrication skill paired with a designer's eye, so a piece looks right and holds up. Whether it's a custom metal sign, a laser-cut gift, or a 3D-printed part, I handle the design and the build myself, start to finish.
